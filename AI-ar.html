معلومات منظمة العفو الدولية

الذكاء الاصطناعي هو مجال في علوم الكمبيوتر يركز على تطوير أنظمة تكنولوجية قادرة على أداء المهام التي تتطلب ذكاءً شبيهاً بالذكاء البشري، مثل التعلم والاستدلال واتخاذ القرار.

  • نحن هنا في مختبرات مدرسة سمهارم نركز على التكامل

    تركز مدرسة سمهرم ثنائية اللغة الخاصة على التطوير الشامل لطلابنا لمهاراتهم الحركية الفكرية والروحية والعاطفية أثناء تعليمهم لاكتشاف قوتهم الداخلية أولاً من خلال التشجيع والممارسة في بيئات تعلم آمنة لحياتهم داخل و خارج المدرسة.

  • الذكاء الاصطناعي

    في سميهرم، قمنا بتشكيل فريق مكون من 3 طلاب تحت إشراف فني المختبر. شاركنا في هاكاثون نماء التنموي لخدمات ظفار. قام الطالبان خالد حسن أحمد تبوك من الصف العاشر وسعيد أشرف سعيد المشيكي من الصف السابع بإنشاء جهاز تنبيه لدرجة الحرارة والرطوبة لغرفة المواد الكيميائية

Labib

Labiba

I'm Labiba, a character created by the lab technician to help her explain scientific material and conduct experiments.

Labortary Chart Room

The laboratory chat room is an activity that helps develop students' thinking and expand their knowledge by giving them complete freedom to ask any scientific question they want to know more about. Students' questions are collected and directly posed to Labeeb or Labeeba, who thoroughly answer them."

Science Cinema

"Science Cinema" is an occasional activity we engage in to attract students and connect their real-life experiences with science. We screen scientific documentaries that combine entertainment with knowledge for the students. A fully equipped hall and meals are provided to give students the real cinema experience.

Labib & Labiba's Friends

"Labeeb and Labeeba's friends are all the students of Samehram School "who are eager for science and passionate about all its branches. For the past four years and until now, fun scientific experiments have been conducted during the students' break every week. These experiments are conducted using simple tools and safe materials that are not dangerous, allowing students to try them at home. Through these experiments, we have noticed a very strong connection between the students and the laboratory, attracting them to the love of exploration and learning."

scientific Innovation and AI

Scientific innovation and artificial intelligence represent two interconnected and rapidly evolving fields in our current era. Scientific innovation refers to the use of ideas and knowledge to find new solutions to challenges and develop technology. As for artificial intelligence, it focuses on developing technological systems and programs capable of performing tasks that require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and decision-making. These fields intersect in many applications, such as medicine, robotics, and data analysis, where artificial intelligence is used to improve scientific processes and enhance innovation efficiency
