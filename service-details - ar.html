A learning environment that simulates multiple types of information sources. The importance of learning resource centers: - It provides a suitable environment that enables the student to use a variety of resources. - Assist in organizing and categorizing educational resources for easy access. - Assists the teacher through the Secretary of the Center in the preparation and implementation of the class and reorganization of educational resources used and arranged and ensure its readiness for the next times. Objectives of Learning Resource Centers. - Support the curriculum by providing learning resources related to the curriculum, in order to stimulate effectiveness. - Develop the skills of research, exploration, thinking and problem solving of the learner. - Providing the learner with the skills and tools that enable him to adapt and benefit from the rapid developments in information systems. - Meet the needs of individual differences by provided Services Learning Resource Center. - Providing different sources of information related to educational needs. - Assist students and teachers and train them to use information sources. - Provide facilities that help the teacher to produce simple teaching aids.
